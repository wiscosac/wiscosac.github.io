We introduced Bradley Terry Models, these can be used for your submission to the [March Madness Competition](/Challenge/SP/MarchMadness).

A Bradley–Terry model is a statistical model used to estimate the relative strength of teams or players by modeling the probability that one teams wins in a pairwise comparison against another.

View [the code](https://github.com/wiscosac/wiscosac.github.io/blob/1255ad8130d62efe8f6ae3f6af44f4aa344d81aa/public/files/bradley-terry-workshop.r) from the workshop.
